UNIX exercises§

  1. Use wc to determine how many lines are in lamina.bed.
  2. Use wc to determine how many characters are in lamina.bed.
  3. Print out the 10 states (1st column, contains spaces) with the highest income (3rd column) from states.tab.
$ wc -l lamina.bed # lines

$ wc -c lamina.bed # characters

$ sort -t'\t' -k3nr states.tab | head -n 10
